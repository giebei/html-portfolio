The Not So Exciting, Exciting Book List

Books Read for 2024

Before Ever After by Samantha Sotto

Pick me up books for easy read and engaging story. Though it might be an aquired taste, it's like mini stories weaved into a bigger picture that's a treat, a history mystery combo. Drink some jasmine tea, as suggested by our protagonists, and maybe some egg related snacks.

Bridgerton by Julie Quinn

Discovered through netflix, but as a bookworm, preferred to read it. Noble idiots of gentlemen that act as boys. And of ladies that have societal expectations to please. Good series for a quick romantic, and dare I say, comidic stories. A classic earl grey, for a classic type of story, with some biscuits! (just don't let it drop on your books).

Murdle by G.T. Karber

Frustratingly wonderful. The puzzles are great, though it might be frustrating if you end up solving the puzzles too long when all you want to do is know the story. Best enjoyed with a strong coffee of your choice, for a clique detective journey you're about to take, maybe some donuts on the side for maximum effect.
